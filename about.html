The Mission

Our Sourcing.

Bridging the gap between Excellence and Global Markets.

MRT International Holding LLC was founded on a simple yet ambitious premise: that high-end, factory-direct goods should be accessible to a global audience without the usual complications of international sourcing.

We leverage a proprietary network of quality-vetted manufacturers and automated logistics pipelines to curate collections that stand the test of time, both in design and durability.
